Air for Free

Relient K

July 22, 2016

Air for Free is the eighth studio album by Relient K, realeased on July 22, 2016, through Mono vs Stereo. The albums's lead single, "Bummin" was released on April 13, 2016.

Band Members

  • Matt Thiessen-Lead vocals, piano, guitar
  • Matt Hoopes-Guitars, omnichord, keys, background vocals
  • Dave Douglas-drums, background vocals
  • Tom Breyfogle-drums, percussion
  • Mark Lee Townsend-Bass, background vocals
  • Mark Lee Townsent-Producer
